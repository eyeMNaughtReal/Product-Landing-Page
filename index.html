Company Story

Founded in 2014, Shenzhen Creality 3D Technology CoCompany. LtdLimited Company. focuses on the design, research, production of 3D printers and 3D printing derivatives. Consisting of professional researchers and skilled engineers, our R&DResearch and Development team constantly strives for quality and excellence. The company’s R&DResearch and Development investments on additive manufacturing cover FDMFused Deposition Modeling / DLPDigital Light Processing / SLAStereolithography 3D printers, 3D scanners, filaments, resin as well as 3D printed drones and robots that back up STEMScience, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics education.

Our factory is certificated with BSCIBusiness Social Compliance Initiative and ISOInternational Organization for Standardization, covering an area of 20,000 square meters, and achieving an annual production capacity of over 500,000 units. Our products are all certified to CEThe Conformitè Europëenne, FCCFederal Communications Commission, and RoHSRestriction of Hazardous Substances standards. We have exported to more than 100 countries including, but not limited to France, the United States of America, Australia, Russia, the United Kingdom, Germany, Singapore, Egypt, and India; bringing technological innovation to all walks of life.

We are helping people Create Reality and Achieve Dreams with affordable consumer 3D printers.
